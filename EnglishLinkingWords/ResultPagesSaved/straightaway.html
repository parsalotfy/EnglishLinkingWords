    B1 immediately:

    We don't have to go straightaway, do we?

    straightaway noun [ C ]
    uk ​ /ˌstreɪt.əˈweɪ/ us ​ /ˌstreɪt̬.əˈweɪ/ US mainly UK straight

    › the straight part of a racetrack (= the track on which competitors race):

    And the runners are just coming up to the straightaway.
