    “expressly” in American English

     See all translations
    expressly adverb
    us ​ /ɪkˈspres·li/

    › in a clear way; in a way that shows intention or choice:

    He wrote the play expressly for her.

    “expressly” in English

     See all translations
    expressly adverb
    uk ​ /ɪkˈspres.li/ us ​ /ɪkˈspres.li/

    › in a way that is clear:

    I expressly told you to be home by midnight.

    › for a particular purpose:

    The farmer put up the fence expressly to stop people walking across his field.
